Dungeon Escape

A severe headache (it feels like someone gave you a good shot on the head) prevents you from remembering how you got here. You hear someone coming to the door and unlocking the lock. The guard tells you to follow him, refusing to explain what's happening. You appear before the young prince of the local empire, who is clearly in high spirits. He offers you a chance to get out of prison if you beat him in his favourite card game.

The rules of the game are pretty simple. You need to choose one of three cards: cavalryman, spearmen and archer. The cavalryman defeats the archer, the archer defeats the spearman, the spearman defeats the cavalryman. The winner is calculated after 5 rounds.
